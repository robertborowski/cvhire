Top 3 Workplace Trivia Platforms That Improve Employee Engagement

Triviafy your workspace.

Does This Sound Like Your Team’s Weekly Routine?

- M: “How was your weekend?”

- T/W: “Can you believe this weather? How was your commute?”

- Th/F: “Any plans for the weekend?”

Results/Cons:

- Company culture suffers = Work quality suffers.

- Team members feel unengaged, robotic, and lacking in personal connection.

- False sense of team camaraderie among coworkers.

Question: How can employee engagement at a typical company be improved?

Answer: Improve Employee Engagement With Trivia Quizzes

Benefits/Pros of weekly team trivia quizzes:

- Discover your team members hidden knowledge and passions.

- Build team camaraderie and strengthen relationships.

- Increase employee satisfaction at your company.

- Reduce stress levels.

- Increase brain performance.

- Improve company PR.

- Test and enhance your memory.

- Earn bragging rights among coworkers.

- Win prizes.

Question: How can I get weekly trivia quizzes sent to my team automatically? Below are some of the leading platforms to help improve employee engagement through trivia.

1. Triviafy

Website: https://triviafy.com/

Summary

- Weekly trivia quizzes sent to your team automatically.

Pros

- 100% Automated: Triviafy creates/grades weekly trivia quizzes automatically.

- Asynchronous: team members submit answers on their own time throughout the week.

- Fully Customizable: Able to create custom trivia questions for your team, select quiz difficulty, create custom quiz categories, customize quiz open and close dates and times.

- Gift Card Prizes: Team members that win quizzes consistently win free gift cards to popular companies like Amazon, Nike, Adidas, etc.

- Flat Monthly Rate: Unlimited users per team for one flat monthly rate.

- First 30 Days are Free: no credit card required, cancel any time.

Cons

- Cheating: No way to prove if an employee is Googling answers on their own time. (If an employee wants to put their own honesty/reputation at risk within their company then that is their own choice).

- Slack Integration Only: Currently Triviafy is 100% focused on Slack integration while Microsoft Teams/Email signups are scheduled to be in production in early 2022.

2. WaterCoolerTrivia

Website: https://www.watercoolertrivia.com/

Summary

- Weekly trivia quizzes sent to your team automatically.

Pros

- 100% Automated: WaterCoolerTrivia creates/grades weekly trivia quizzes automatically.

- Asynchronous: team members submit answers on their own time throughout the week.

- Fully Customizable: Able to create custom trivia questions for your team, select quiz difficulty, create custom quiz categories, customize quiz open and close dates and times.

- Slack, Microsoft Teams, Email: WaterCoolerTrivia has both Slack and Microsoft Teams integrations as well as Email signups available.

- First 4 Quizzes are Free: no credit card required, cancel any time.

Cons

- Cheating: No way to prove if an employee is Googling answers on their own time. (If an employee wants to put their own honesty/reputation at risk within their company then that is their own choice).

- No Prizes = Less Incentive: Currently, at the time this article was written, WaterCoolerTrivia’s website states that they are a “prize-less trivia contest.”

- Fluctuating Monthly Rate: Depending on how many people are on your team your monthly rate differs. When you increase the size of your team you likely have to get approval from your boss to increase the monthly subscription cost.

3. SpringWorks - Trivia

Website: https://www.springworks.in/trivia/

Summary

- Friendly workplace/office games platform. Trivia/Games include but are not limited to: Instant Quizzes, (Un)popular Opinion, Word Puzzle, This or That, Peer Introductions, Pictionary.

Pros

- Variety of Workplace Games: Employees are less likely to grow bored of one quiz type/game and can interact with another.

- Cheating is Less Likely: Team members are completing trivia quizzes at the same time and are less likely to be Googling answers in front of each other.

- Fully Customizable: Able to create custom trivia questions/quizzes for your team.

Cons

- Variety of Workplace Games: Employees can become distracted by the amount of games available.

- Synchronous: The majority of games/trivia quizzes require all team members to participate at the same time.

- Fluctuating Monthly Rate: Depending on how many people are on your team your monthly rate differs. When you increase the size of your team you likely have to get approval from your boss to increase the monthly subscription cost.

Conclusion

Weekly workplace trivia is a great way to engage employees and strengthen co-worker relationships.

Asynchronous trivia quizzes work best because they allow employees to complete questions on their own time which does not interfere with company workflows.

Weekly trivia allows team members to get to know each other on a more personal level and conversations become less robotic in the workplace.

Finally, weekly trivia quizzes help make your company unique.
