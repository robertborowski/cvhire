What is Triviafy?

Slack app Triviafy is a weekly trivia quiz for your team. The weekly trivia quiz is automated and asynchronous.

Automated: No logistical headaches for you. Quizzes are automatically created, announced, distributed, and graded on a weekly basis.

Asynchronous: Coworkers complete the quiz on their own time throughout the week. Your weekly schedule will not be affected by the weekly trivia quiz.

Custom Trivia Quiz

Triviafy is fully customizable. You, and your team members, can change:

- Quiz timeframe,

- Number of questions,

- Question difficulty,

- Create custom questions,

- Custom question categories, department specific trivia.

Trivia Custom Question Categories, Department Specific Trivia

With Triviafy your team can customize quiz question categories to be department specific.

Example 1: You’re on the engineering team and your team only wants engineering related trivia questions on their weekly quizzes. Simply select the category: “Engineering” in Settings > Quiz Settings.

Example 2: You’re on the marketing team and your team wants marketing, pop culture, and movie related trivia questions on their weekly quizzes. Simply select the categories: “Marketing” AND “Pop Culture” AND “Movies” in Settings > Quiz Settings.

By default all trivia categories are already selected but you, and your team members, can change the trivia quiz categories to be department specific or more broad week over week.

Trivia Learning and Development

By narrowing the range of question categories, Triviafy can be used as a learning and development tool for your team.
