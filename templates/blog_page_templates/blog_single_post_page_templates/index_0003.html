Top 5 Free Slack Apps For Team Building and Employee Engagement

Triviafy your workspace.

Overview

List of best free trial Slack apps that automate and outsource team building activities and employee engagement initiatives.

Team building activities without the logistical headaches. Give your employees something to talk about besides the weather.

1. Triviafy

Triviafy is a weekly trivia quiz sent directly to your team.

Slack App: Free and paid plans available.

2. Donut

Donut is a serendipitous water cooler conversation starter tool that connects team members.

Slack App: Free and paid plans available.

3. BirthdayBot

BirthdayBot helps you keep track of office birthdays and anniversaries.

Slack App: Free and paid plans available.

4. Polly

Polly is a poll, survey, check-in, onboarding feedback, social games tool.

Slack App: Free and paid plans available.

5. Icebreaker Time

Icebreaker Time is an icebreaker question generating tool.

Slack App: Free and paid plans available.
