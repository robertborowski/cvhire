Slack Setup Guide

Triviafy your workspace.

1. Slack, click “Add channels.”

Note: If a team mate has already invited you to a custom Slack channel then skip to steps 5-7.

2. Click “Create a channel.”

Note: If a team mate has already invited you to a custom Slack channel then skip to steps 5-7.

3. Create channel name.

Recommended: Make private.

Note: If a team mate has already invited you to a custom Slack channel then skip to steps 5-7.

4. Invite team members to channel.

Note: If a team mate has already invited you to a custom Slack channel then skip to steps 5-7.

*5. Each team member:

Go to Triviafy > “Create Account” > “Add to Slack.”

6. Slack OAuth, click “Search for a channel...”

Triviafy is a 100% Slack approved app. Learn more about Slack authorizations.

7. Select Slack channel.

Select the Slack channel you created or were invited to by a team member.

All set! Good luck on your weekly L&D team building quiz.
