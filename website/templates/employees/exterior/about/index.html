Triviafy is a pre-employment testing platform that helps employers identify candidates that have the skills and abilities to best fit the job requirements. Triviafy offers pre-employment assessments for 50+ languages, tools, and frameworks. Some of the most popular assessments focus on skills like Excel, Python, JavaScript, and SQL. Triviafy also offers assessments based on soft skills such as teamwork, problem solving, communication, adaptability, critical thinking, and time management.

Triviafy’s platform allows you to use pre-built assessments or create your own custom assessments from scratch. Customize your assessment’s questions, categories, frameworks, difficulty, timeframe, passing score, and more. You can create a custom triviafy assessment and send it to a candidate in less than 2 minutes.

Triviafy offers in depth candidate analytics to provide you with even more insight into the effectiveness of each candidate that completes a test through our platform. Triviafy helps you understand which candidate has the widest array of skills as well as a candidates ranking by skill.
