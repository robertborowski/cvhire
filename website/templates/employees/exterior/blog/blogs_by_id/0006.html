Ice Breaker Questions — Top Ice Breaker Tools & Apps For Engaging Employees 2022

View Medium article

Published: Jan 27, 2022

Define Ice Breaker

According to Drexel.edu “Any short activity that requires the participants to break into teams to conduct the activity is a team building ice breaker.”

Continued: “They are particularly beneficial to new teams, teams in which there has been dysfunction, or teams that have experienced significant leadership changes.”

Below are some of the top 5 Ice Breaker tools available today.

1. Ice Shaker

Ice Shaker is a series of quick icebreaker games that will get your team talking and interacting with one another in a much more personal way.

2. Ricotta

Ricotta offers ice breaker games and activities directly within Slack channels.

3. Triviafy

Triviafy is a fun weekly trivia quiz that helps teams get to know one another better in just 2 minutes per week.

4. WaterCoolerTrivia

WaterCoolerTrivia is a fun weekly trivia quiz that helps teams get to know one another better in just 2 minutes per week.

5. Friday

Friday is an app that allows you to utilize ice breakers and custom questions as part of workflows.
