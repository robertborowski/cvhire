Question: What is hybrid work?

A hybrid work schedule is a combination of working remotely and working in office.

Problem: How does hybrid work impact my team?

Many companies are adopting the hybrid work model and pushing employees to work in office for longer than 60% of the time.

Impact: Mandatory participation in this *return to office policy* is likely to cause unhappy and unengaged team members.

Solution: List of best hybrid team building activities.

You can help make the hybrid work model enjoyable with the following team building activities:

1. Triviafy

Triviafy is a weekly trivia quiz sent directly to your team.

  • Asynchronous: employees complete the weekly Triviafy quiz on their own time.
  • Automated: Quiz creation, grading, weekly winner rewards are 100% automated.
  • Free Trial: 30 days / 4 weeks of free Triviafy quizzes takes less than 50 seconds to set up.

2. Pinturillo 2

Pinturillo 2 is a timed drawing and word guessing game.

  • Art Skills: Showcase your artistic skills to coworkers.
  • Free to play online.

3. Donut

Donut introduces teammates for virtual coffees or post engaging prompts in communication channels.

  • Culture of camaraderie: Coworkers can develop new, more personable, relationships and conversations.
  • Free and Paid features.
