12 Team Building Activities That Your Team Won't Hate

Published: June 1, 2023

Overview

Team building is essential for fostering collaboration, boosting morale, and strengthening relationships among team members. Whether your team is working in person or remotely, there are numerous activities that can help bring everyone together. In this blog post, we will explore 12 exciting team building activities that can be seamlessly conducted in both in-person and virtual settings. Additionally, we will delve into the benefits of using Triviafy, a team building software, to enhance your team building efforts.

12 Team Building Activities

1. Two Truths and a Lie

Encourage team members to share interesting facts about themselves, challenging others to identify the false statement.

2. Scavenger Hunt

Create a list of items or tasks for participants to find or complete, either physically or virtually, fostering teamwork and problem-solving skills.

3. Virtual Office Trivia

Engage your team in a fun and competitive trivia game using platforms like Triviafy, where participants can showcase their knowledge and learn more about your company or industry.

4. Human Knot

Form a circle and have team members hold hands with two different people across the circle. Together, they must unravel the human knot without letting go, promoting communication and teamwork.

5. Online Escape Room

Collaborate virtually to solve puzzles, find clues, and escape from a virtual room within a time limit, developing problem-solving and critical thinking skills.

6. Storytelling Circle

Create a shared story by having each team member contribute a sentence or two, encouraging creativity and building a sense of unity.

7. Team Cook-Off

In-person, divide the team into smaller groups and assign them a dish to prepare. Virtually, team members can cook the same dish in their own kitchens, sharing their culinary creations over video conferencing.

8. Marshmallow Challenge

Provide teams with materials like spaghetti, tape, string, and a marshmallow, and task them with building the tallest freestanding structure, promoting innovation and collaboration.

9. Guess the Emoji

Share a series of emojis or emoticons representing words, phrases, or movie titles, encouraging participants to decipher the meaning within a set timeframe.

10. Picture Puzzle

Transform a team photo or an image representing the team into a jigsaw puzzle, distributing pieces among team members to assemble virtually or in person.

11. Collaborative Playlist

Foster a sense of shared experiences by having team members suggest songs that motivate or inspire them, creating a collaborative playlist on platforms like Spotify.

12. Appreciation Circle

Encourage team members to express their appreciation for one another's contributions, skills, or positive qualities, promoting a positive and supportive team culture.
