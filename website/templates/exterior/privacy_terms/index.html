Privacy Policy

Welcome to CVhire, your trusted CV screening platform. We understand the importance of privacy, especially when it comes to your personal data. 2. Use of Your Information

Your data is primarily used for analyzing and comparing CVs to job descriptions using our AI technology, helping determine if the candidate's resume/CV is a good match for the job description.
