Top 5 Team Building Activities for Remote Teams 2022 New Year

Triviafy your workspace.

Overview

To combat “The Great Resignation” real leaders turn to team building activities.

We’ve listed some of our favorite team building activities, employee engagement initiatives, learning and development tools.

Benefit: L&D, team building initiatives without the logistical headaches.

1. Triviafy

Triviafy is a weekly trivia quiz sent directly to your team. Weekly trivia is asynchronous, meaning each coworker completes the quiz on their own time and winner’s are announced at the end of the week.

Slack approved app, setup takes less than 30 seconds.

2. Kahoot!

Kahoot! is a global learning platform company that wants to empower everyone, including children, students, and employees, to unlock their full learning potential.

Kahoot! is used in many institutions of higher education around the world, including 87% of the global top 500 universities.

3. Workplace

Workplace is a communication tool that connects everyone in your company, even if they’re working remotely. Use familiar features such as News Feed, Chat, Live video and Groups, to get people talking and working together.

Workplace is a Meta company.

4. Skribbl

Skribbl is a multiplayer drawing and guessing game. One game consists of a few rounds in which every round someone has to draw their chosen word and others have to guess it to gain points.

5. QuizBreaker

QuizBreaker is a scheduled virtual team building quiz delivered to your inbox. Helping teams bond, no matter where they are.
