Privacy & Security

Private Data

We respect user data privacy and only collect the necessary information to provide our services. We never sell or rent user data to third parties and only share it with trusted service providers as necessary.

Encrypt Data

We use strong encryption methods to protect user data both in transit and at rest. This ensures that user data is secure and cannot be accessed by unauthorized parties.

Own Your Data

Users own their data and can delete it at any time. We believe in giving users full control over their personal information and respecting their right to data privacy.
